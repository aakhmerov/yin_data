About us

We are visualizing growth rate of real estate properties over a selected area of time. This can be either the past or the future!

We offer two different features:

  1. Show historical information on how prices changed for properties in selected areas.
  2. Show predictions based on historical data on how prices going to change in the future

Based on our predictions investors can see right away in which locations it is most likely to buy real estates in order to sell them with benefit in the future.

You can use a slider to show different time in history or in the future to the avarage growth rate of a certain area.

Green squares indicate increase in value, red squares indicate a decrease.
